About Me

I was born and raised in Riverton, Utah and I am the oldest of 7 kids. I love the outdoors, sports, and spending time with my wife and family. Whenever the BYU football team is playing you can bet that’ll be watching the game at my in-laws. We currently live in Provo while my wife finishes up her BS degree from Utah Valley University.

I graduated from BYU Marriot School of Business in December of 2020 with my undergrad in Experience and Design Management. This degree focused on creating a positive journey for customers, CX Design, UX Design, management, and social media management and marketing.

After graduating from Brigham Young University, I decided to make a change and study software engineering by attending a 6-month bootcamp offered by the University of Utah. I have always enjoyed creating and designing and I discovered that web development could be an exciting career to get into that would allow me to create and use my other skills to better the lives of others and provide a good life for my family.

Feel free to reaching out to me about career opportunities or just connect on LinkedIn!
